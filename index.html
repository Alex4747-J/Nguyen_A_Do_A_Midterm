Gryffindor vs Slytherin

The Story about house Slytherin

  • The house colours were silver and green, and a serpent emblem. The Slytherin common room was located in the castle's dungeons.
  • House Slytherin founder was Salazar Slytherin, one of the most a talented wizard, and skilled in Parseltongue and Legilimency.
  • The characteristics defined by cunning, pride and ambition.
  • Alas, Salazar's anti-Muggle stance and belief that Hogwarts should be for pure-blooded students only gave the house a poor reputation.

Advantages of house Slytherin

  • Ambition and Resourcefulness: Slytherins are known for their strong ambition and resourcefulness, enabling them to set and achieve high goals through strategic planning and determination.
  • Strategic Decision-Making: Unlike Gryffindors, who may act impulsively, Slytherins are more calculated, carefully evaluating potential outcomes before taking action, leading to more effective decisions.
  • Adaptability and Self-Preservation: Slytherins excel in assessing situations and adjusting their strategies to ensure favorable outcomes, showcasing flexibility and resilience in various circumstances.

Slytherins, of course, have quite a bit of “honor,” at least in their own opinions. They have a lot of pride. They also are conscious of how they are viewed, but in a rather “snobby” way. Instead of having a need to prove them, they want to look sophisticated; thus, they have “honor” in a more selfish way.

Why is Slytherin a better house in my opinion

  • Slytherine sets a high goal and doesn't stop until they achieve them. Their drive and resourcefulness make them natural leaders and innovators.
  • Unlike 3 other houses, Slytherin uses their intelligence strategically. They think ahead, plan carefully and always stay a step ahead.
  • In my opinion, although Slytherin is seen as cold, they are fiercely loyal to those they trust. Their inner circle, like their family, will do anything to protect their own.
  • In wizarding world history, many influential wizards such as Severus Sanape, Merlin and even Voldemort, aka Tom Marvolo Riddle (though not exactly a role model) came from Slytherin. The house breeds power, leadership and lasting impacts.
